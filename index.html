About Nova Dev Studio

We started Nova Dev Studio because we saw a problem: Local service businesses—cleaners, plumbers, electricians, salons, and tradespeople—were losing jobs to competitors with online presence. Many tried expensive agencies or built websites that looked pretty but didn't generate a single lead.

We built a different approach. We focus on what actually matters: a clean, professional website that shows up in Google, builds trust with customers, and turns visitors into phone calls.

Today, we help South African service businesses get online without the complexity, the long timelines, or the corporate price tags.
